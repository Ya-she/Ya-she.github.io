Privacy

No account is required to play What MBI Are You?.

Quiz progress

The quiz does not store your answers or result after you leave or refresh the page.

Cookies, analytics, and accounts

This version does not use quiz-progress cookies, user accounts, logins, advertising trackers, or analytics code.

External content

Some quiz images may load from external image hosts. Those services may receive standard technical information from your browser, such as your IP address and browser type.

Clearing data

Because quiz progress is not stored, there is no quiz-progress data to clear.
