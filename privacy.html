Privacy

No account is required to play What MBI Are You?.

Saved progress

The quiz saves your progress in a browser cookie on your own device. This stores your selected answers, current question, and result so you can return later.

Analytics and accounts

This version does not include user accounts, logins, advertising trackers, or analytics code.

External content

Some quiz images may load from external image hosts. Those services may receive standard technical information from your browser, such as your IP address and browser type.

Clearing data

You can use the “Clear saved progress” or “Clear saved result” buttons on the quiz page to remove the quiz cookie from your browser.
