About What MBI Are You?

What MBI Are You? is a playful personality quiz about market-based instruments, environmental policy, and the strange ways institutions try to make nature legible to markets.

The quiz is meant for entertainment and light satire. The result is not a policy recommendation, personality diagnosis, or scientific classification.

The site is a free browser game created by Obliqua Data with expert MBI knowledge from Felice Diekel and Yashi Punia.
